Our Culinary Journey

Luxury Recipe Finder was founded in 2018 by a team of passionate chefs and food enthusiasts. What began as a small recipe blog has grown into a premier culinary platform serving millions of food lovers worldwide.

Our journey started with a simple mission: to make gourmet cooking accessible to everyone. Today, we continue that mission by partnering with world-class chefs and culinary experts to bring you the finest recipes from around the globe.

2018

Founded in a small Parisian kitchen with just 50 recipes

2020

2022

Featured in Michelin Guide as top recipe platform

2025

Serving over 5 million monthly users worldwide
